How it works

From overwhelmed to automated in 4 steps.

A clear, predictable process. No mystery, no jargon, no months-long discovery phase. Most builds take two to four weeks from kickoff to launch.

Book a consultation
1

Discovery — we map your workflows

Before we touch a tool, we get to know your business. We sit down (virtually) with you and the people doing the work, walk through a normal day, and identify where the time really goes. We list the tools you already use, the tasks that get repeated most, and the moments where balls get dropped. By the end, you've got a clear picture of your top automation opportunities — even if you decide not to work with us.

You leave with: a written workflow map and a shortlist of high-leverage automations.

2

Design — we identify the highest-leverage automations

Not every task should be automated. Some are too small, some are too sensitive, some need a human in the loop. In this step, we pick the one or two automations that will free up the most time fastest — and design how the agent should behave, what data it touches, and where humans stay involved. You get a plan that's specific, scoped, and priced.

You leave with: a custom build plan, scope, and a clear price.

3

Build & Train — custom agents + your team learns to use them

We build the agent, connect it to your tools, and tune it on real examples from your business. Then we train your team — live walkthroughs, short reference docs, and clear escalation paths for when the agent runs into something unusual. We don't go live until you and your team feel confident.

You leave with: a live AI agent, trained team, and reference docs.

4

Support — ongoing tuning + new agents as you grow

The first 30 days are the most important. We monitor how the agent behaves with real customers and real edge cases, and tune it based on what we learn. After that, you choose your support tier. Some clients want light-touch monthly check-ins. Others want us building their next three agents. Either works.

You leave with: a partner who understands your stack and helps it evolve.

Timeline

Most builds take 2–4 weeks.

Discovery is usually 3–5 days. Design is 2–3 days. Build and training make up the bulk of the time. Then we go live and stay in close contact for the first month.

FAQ

Process questions, answered.

What does the discovery call cover?
A walkthrough of how your business runs day-to-day, what tools you already use, and where time gets lost. We come prepared with questions but mostly listen. There is no obligation to move forward.
Do you work with my existing tools?
Almost always, yes. We connect to whatever you're already using — email, CRM, calendar, voice, ticketing, spreadsheets — through official APIs. We don't ask you to rip and replace what's working.
How involved does my team need to be?
During discovery, we need a few hours from you and the person closest to the work. During build, almost zero involvement. During training, expect 1–2 short sessions per affected role.
What happens if the agent makes a mistake?
Every agent is designed with clear escalation rules — it asks for human help when things look unusual. During the 30-day tuning window we use those edge cases to make the agent smarter.
