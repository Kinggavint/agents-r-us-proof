Illustrative case study — anonymized

Service business → lead follow-up agent

Client profile: 8-person plumbing company

Challenge: Inbound leads were piling up. Owners were missing callbacks during jobs, losing booked work to whichever competitor called back first.

What we built: An AI agent that watches inbound calls, texts, and form submissions; replies within minutes; qualifies the job; and books an appointment directly into the team calendar.

Illustrative case study — anonymized

Professional firm → CRM + email automation

Client profile: 15-person consultancy

Challenge: Consultants were spending hours every week typing notes, updating CRM records, and scheduling follow-ups. The work was getting done — but it was the most expensive admin work in the building.

What we built: An AI agent that reads email threads, updates CRM records automatically, and schedules follow-ups based on what was discussed. The team approves outgoing replies; AI handles the rest.
