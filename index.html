Frequently asked

Common questions, plain answers.

What is an AI agent?
An AI agent is a small program that can read, decide, and act on its own — like an assistant that handles a specific job. Examples: an agent that watches your inbox and replies to leads, or one that fills your CRM from email threads. They run in the background and only ask for help when something is unusual.
How long does setup take?
Most builds take 2 to 4 weeks. That includes discovery, the build itself, training your team, and an initial tuning window so the agent works well in your specific environment.
Do I need technical skills?
No. We handle the build and the connections to your existing tools. Your team learns how to use the agent and what to do when it asks for help — that's it.
What's the cost?
We don't sell licenses or packaged software, so pricing depends on what you need automated. We share a clear scope and a single price after the discovery call. See our pricing approach.
How do you ensure my data stays private?
We connect to your existing systems through their official APIs, scope every agent to only the data it needs, and walk you through exactly what is read or written before anything goes live. Nothing leaves your environment without your approval.
