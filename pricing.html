FAQ

Pricing model questions.

Why don't you list prices on the site?
Because every build is different. The same agent that takes one week for a 5-person team might take three for a 30-person team with more tools to connect. We share a clear, single price after the discovery call — and it stays fixed.
Is the discovery call really free?
Yes. There's no fee, and no obligation. You leave with a workflow map and a shortlist of automation opportunities even if you decide not to move forward.
What does ongoing support cost?
It depends on how much you want — from light-touch monthly check-ins to fully managed. We discuss this in the design phase so it fits your budget and growth plans.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes — most engagements are billed in milestones (e.g., kickoff, build, launch). We can work with most reasonable schedules.
