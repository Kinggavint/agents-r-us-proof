About Agents R Us

We make AI simple, useful, and customized.

Most AI tools feel either overwhelming or pointlessly generic. We exist for small business owners who know AI could help — but don't have time to figure out how to set it up.

Our approach

Custom-built, practical, and supported.

We build AI agents around your actual business — your tools, your processes, your customers. Not a one-size-fits-all SaaS tool that you bend your workflow to fit.

That means our work starts with listening. We map how you really run the day before we suggest a single automation. The goal is the highest-leverage AI for your team, not the shiniest one.

And we stick around. Setup, training, and ongoing support are part of every engagement — because an AI agent that no one knows how to maintain is just a forgotten tool six months later.

Methodology

How every engagement is structured.

1

Discover

We learn your workflows, tools, and where the time goes today.

2

Customize

We design AI agents around your actual processes — not generic templates.

3

Train

4

Support

Ongoing tuning, fixes, and new automations as your business grows.

Who we work with

Built for small businesses and growing teams.

Busy owners and managers who know AI could help — but don't have the time or technical bandwidth to set it up alone.

Small service businesses

Professional firms

Consultancies, agencies, accounting practices, and law firms drowning in admin work.

Growing teams

10–50 person companies where the operations are getting too big for spreadsheets.

Why we started Agents R Us

Because small businesses deserve the same AI leverage as enterprises.

Big companies have entire teams to roll out AI. Small businesses don't — and the off-the-shelf tools usually miss the mark, because every small business runs a little differently.

We started Agents R Us to close that gap. Custom AI agents, built around how your business actually works, with the setup and support that makes them stick.
